**Thanks for your interest in the development of EventViz**

The EventViz development model is community-based. Users willing to participate are encouraged to fork EventViz Git repository and to submit pull requests for improvements and bug fixes. The EventViz core development team is in charge of the maintenance, releases, and general support to users via the issues system.

Questions, suggestions and bug reports can be made through the [issues system](https://github.com/ammelsayed/EventViz/issues). After checking that the issue is not already being addressed, you can [open an issue](https://github.com/ammelsayed/EventViz/issues/new). Note that you will have to register first.
